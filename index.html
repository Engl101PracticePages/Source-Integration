  3. Source 3 (PDF you uploaded): Job Finder Posting — First Responder (PDF)
Easy MLA tip (for websites + PDFs)

If there are no page numbers, that’s okay. For MLA in-text citations, you can usually use:

  • Organization name: (Bureau of Labor Statistics)
  • Or a short title in quotation marks: (“Job Finder Posting — Postsecondary”)
  • Whatever you choose, match it in your Works Cited.
